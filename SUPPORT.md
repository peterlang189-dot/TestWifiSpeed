## Healthy charging limitation

The app can monitor battery level while it is running and alert you at a configurable threshold. Apple does not expose a public iOS API that lets third-party apps cut charging power, so you must unplug the charger when alerted. iOS may suspend the app in the background; use the system's Optimized Battery Charging feature as the primary protection.

## 健康充电限制

App 可以在运行期间监测电量，并在达到自定义上限时提醒你。Apple 没有向第三方 App 提供切断 iOS 充电电源的公开 API，因此收到提醒后需要手动拔下充电器。iOS 可能暂停后台 App，请将系统“优化电池充电”作为主要保护措施。
