# Test WiFi Speed Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Test WiFi Speed is a network measurement utility. It does not contain advertising or analytics SDKs, does not create user profiles, and does not track users across apps or websites.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores your language, appearance, healthy-charging preferences, and up to eight recent speed-test results in Apple `UserDefaults`. It reads battery level and charging state locally to provide charge-limit alerts. Battery information is not transmitted or retained as history. This information remains on your device. You can delete the saved speed-test results with the **Clear** action in the app. Removing the app also removes its locally stored data.

## Data processed during a speed test

When you start a test, the app downloads and uploads test payloads through Cloudflare's HTTPS speed-test service. Cloudflare receives and processes:

- the public IP address used for the connection;
- an estimated country and city derived from that IP address;
- the Autonomous System Number (ASN) associated with the network; and
- network performance measurements generated by the test.

Cloudflare states that it truncates IP addresses used for its speed test and may share anonymized measurement information with measurement partners for Internet performance research. Test WiFi Speed does not receive your IP address or estimated location from Cloudflare and does not operate a server that stores test results.

Cloudflare processes this information under its [Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/policies/privacy/). A test uses approximately 29 MB of data with the current app configuration. If a cellular connection is active, the app displays a cellular-data warning before starting.

## Purpose, retention, and sharing

Network requests are made only to provide the speed-test and connection-advice features. The developer does not sell personal data or share it for advertising. Local test history is retained until you clear it or remove the app. Cloudflare's retention and deletion practices are governed by its privacy policy.

## Your choices

Speed testing is optional. You may decline the disclosure shown before the first test, cancel a running test, clear local history at any time, or uninstall the app. No account is required.

# Test WiFi Speed 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 12 日

Test WiFi Speed 是网络测量工具，不包含广告或分析 SDK，不建立用户画像，也不会跨 App 或网站跟踪用户。

## 保存在设备上的数据

App 使用 Apple `UserDefaults` 保存语言、外观、健康充电偏好以及最多八条最近测速结果。App 会在本地读取电量与充电状态以提供充电上限提醒；电池信息不会传输，也不会作为历史记录保存。这些信息仅保存在设备上。你可以使用 App 内的“清除”操作删除测速历史；卸载 App 也会删除本地数据。

## 测速期间处理的数据

开始测速后，App 会通过 Cloudflare 的 HTTPS 测速服务下载和上传测试数据。Cloudflare 会接收和处理：

- 当前连接使用的公网 IP 地址；
- 根据 IP 地址推断的国家和城市；
- 当前网络对应的自治系统编号（ASN）；
- 测速产生的网络性能数据。

Cloudflare 表示会截断测速过程中收到的 IP 地址，并可能为互联网性能研究向测量合作伙伴共享匿名测量信息。Test WiFi Speed 不会从 Cloudflare 获取你的 IP 地址或推断位置，也不运营用于保存测速结果的服务器。

Cloudflare 根据其[隐私政策](https://www.cloudflare.com/policies/privacy/)处理这些信息。按照当前配置，一次测速约使用 29 MB 流量；使用蜂窝网络时，App 会在测速前显示流量提醒。

## 用途、保留与共享

网络请求仅用于提供测速和网络分析功能。开发者不会出售个人数据，也不会将其用于广告。本地测速历史会一直保留，直到你主动清除或卸载 App。Cloudflare 的保留和删除规则以其隐私政策为准。

## 你的选择

测速完全自愿。你可以拒绝首次测速前的披露、取消正在运行的测试、随时清除本地历史或卸载 App。App 不要求注册账号。
